LOTR Easter Egg - Design Notes

- Trigger: Player must collect the special ring item in the Volcano region.
- Behavior: When 10 seconds into the Volcano Region, the ring changes enemy behavior and event occurs.
- Sauron Eye NPC: Appears in the Volcano map, follows the player and gets destroyed when the ring is destroyed.
- Ending: Player must Press E at the volcano during event to destroy the ring and event ends.
